Projects

MEMORYNOW

Together with a team of 4 members, We created a platform for the Official Primary School "Fidel Velázquez" - USAER to reduce and take advantage of the time spent in the rehabilitation of people with intellectual deficits. This project was deserving of the 1st place in the Prototypes 2017 contest carried out by the IPN. I helped develop the entire platform from the website, mobile application including design documents, technical manual, user manual and system tests. It was made using Javascript, PHP, MySQL, JQwery and Kotlin.

INFINITY ROOM

At the MxHacks Hackathon I made, together with a comprehensive team, a computer game for blind people, which helps them improve their auditory and psychomotor systems, this game was awarded second place in the hackathon and first in the Facebook challenge. It was made using Javascript, P5JS, Node.js and Speech To Voice.
